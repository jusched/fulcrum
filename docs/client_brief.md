# Client Brief

## What This Solution Does
This workflow turns a meeting transcript into a short executive package that leadership can review quickly. Instead of someone manually rewriting notes after each meeting, the system prepares:
- an executive summary
- 3 high-priority objectives
- 3 concrete action items
- next steps
- an editable slide deck

## Why It Helps
- Reduces manual follow-up work after recurring meetings
- Makes action owners and priorities clearer
- Creates more consistent leadership communication
- Preserves the reasoning behind recommendations instead of handing over a black-box summary

## How A Team Would Use It
1. Upload a meeting recording or transcript into a shared folder.
2. The workflow processes the file and drafts the summary package.
3. A human reviews the output.
4. After approval, the package is shared with leadership.

## Manual Steps That Remain
- Confirm the meeting input is complete.
- Review the generated summary before distribution.
- Edit wording when business context or tone needs adjustment.

## Cadence
This is designed for recurring meetings such as weekly leadership reviews, operations calls, or project checkpoints.

## Why Human Review Stays In The Loop
The workflow is built to save time, not to replace judgment. Recommendations should be checked before they are shared broadly, especially when business priorities change or transcript quality is weak.
